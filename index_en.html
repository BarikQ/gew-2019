170+ countries are involved in Global Entrepreneurship Week

  • Events.
     Global Entrepreneurship Week in Belarus is a week of diverse events for business . They are important for SMEs, startups, ecosystem builders, corporations, as well as for a new generation of entrepreneurs. On the main stage of the Marriott Hotel Minsk we meet international speakers from global companies with unique cases of business transformation, development of innovative products and scaling companies.
  • EXPO.
     Global Entrepreneurship Week in Belarus is a week of diverse events for business. They are important for SMEs, startups, ecosystem builders, corporations, as well as for a new generation of entrepreneurs. On the main stage of the Marriott Hotel Minsk we meet international speakers from global companies with unique cases of business transformation, development of innovative products and scaling companies.
  • Startups.
     Global Entrepreneurship Week in Belarus provides an opportunity for startups to prepare booths at the Startup Alley, participate in a startup competition to secure a chance to go to a study tour in the UK, meet investors and partners as part of high-quality networking.
